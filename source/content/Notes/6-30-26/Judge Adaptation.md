Appealing to the judge doesn't mean fine-tuning yourself to what the judge wants; it's about *generally* appealing to the judge. 
1. Sound persuasive. It doesn't matter if you're following their paradigm if you don't sound persuasive.
2. Be as *generally* likeable as possible, even out of round.
3. Form relationships with judges and prioritize RFDs. If you get a repeat judge (or your friend has been judged by them, to a lesser extent), look back at the RFD and see what to improve on.
4. Sounding like you're **winning** makes you a **winner**
	1. A pretty speaking voice, confidence, and a **good ballot story** ensures consistent success
	2. You want someone to watch you and think, "oh, they're good"
