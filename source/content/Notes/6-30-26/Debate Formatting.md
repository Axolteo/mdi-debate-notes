# Pocket
The pocket is basically just the title of your document. 
## Hat
The next level down is the hat. It's the first suborganization option; differentiates case, blocks, shells, extensions, etc...
### Tag
Then, we have the tag. It's the tagline of the card; what you read in round to summarize the card and is a single line that connects to your argument. It can also be used for subpoints.
